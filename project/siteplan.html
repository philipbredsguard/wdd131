Site Name

Stellaris University of Aeronautics

I chose this name because "Stellaris" evokes the vastness of space and the high standards of an elite educational institution dedicated to flight and exploration.

stellaris-university.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to serve as a digital hub for prospective students and researchers to explore our aerospace engineering programs, view specialized course catalogs, and connect with our admissions team.

Scenarios

  • "What specific courses are offered in the Satellite Propulsion track?"
  • "Where can I find the contact information for the graduate admissions office?"

Color Schema

Primary: #0B1D33 - Used for headers and footers.
Secondary: #FF6B35 - Used for buttons and highlights.
Accent: #F8F9FA - Used for background sections.

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Orbitron' (or a clean Sans-Serif like 'Roboto') - Used for all H1 and H2 tags to give a high-tech feel.
  • Body Font: 'Inter' (or 'Open Sans') - Used for all paragraph and list text for high readability.
